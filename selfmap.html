Self Mapping

A "self-portrait" website using Google Maps data, presenting fragmented experiences and memories through a digital collage.

The page elements represent saved places—restaurants, museums, music venues—and frequently visited spots like friends' homes, school, and workspace. By collaging them, I visualize my preferences and daily life.

This interactive one-page site allows users to explore "the digital me" through pop-ups and sounds, like assembling a puzzle. I used Photoshop for photo editing, Premiere Pro for video and sound editing, and JavaScript for interactive elements, making the experience immersive and dynamic.

Visit the live website: https://lsyokoo.github.io/selfmapping/
