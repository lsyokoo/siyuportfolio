Step in Elegance:
Fan-Inspired Shoe Designs

Basics: Daily Collection

Inspired by Daniel Rozin's fan installation, this project explores the unique shapes and folding mechanisms of fans, blending traditional elements with contemporary design concepts. The project launched a series of designs ranging from daily wear to runway collections. Each design follows a rigorous process, including research, sketching, and shell sketches on plastic molds. Procreate and Adobe Photoshop are employed for image retouching and drawing.
