True or False Chatbot

In an era overwhelmed by AI-generated and rampant misinformation, discerning the truth becomes increasingly challenging.

This Fact Check Chatbot provides a reliable platform where users can instantly verify the authenticity of the content they encounter online. It evaluates the credibility of both visual and textual data, ensuring users are well-informed and protected from misleading information.

The user-friendly interface makes the verification process seamless and accessible to all, promoting a more informed and responsible online community.
